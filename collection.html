Collection XVI
Collection XVI is a performance oriented creative expression inviting to further explore raw, experimental aesthetics. Designed to overcome the limitations of regular clothing through signature patterns allowing a full range of movement, responding to the needs of modern urban life.
By partnering with the most advanced manufacturers from Switzerland, USA and Italy we are able to offer products in a wide selection of fabrics. This season brings new solutions as we introduce a range of graphene infused garments. New dye experiments and fabric processing techniques are developed to further research new patterns, shapes and textures for our weatherproof materials.
Striving to find the perfect balance between form and functionality, collection XVI introduces further menswear developments.
